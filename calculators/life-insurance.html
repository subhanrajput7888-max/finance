How the Life Insurance Calculator Works

This calculator helps determine an appropriate life insurance coverage amount based on your financial obligations and family needs. The calculation considers:

  • Your annual income and how many years of income replacement is needed
  • Your annual expenses that would need to be covered
  • The number of dependents who rely on your income

The recommended coverage amount is designed to replace your income and cover expenses for the specified period, helping ensure your family's financial security in your absence.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much life insurance do I need?
A common rule of thumb is 10-12 times your annual income, but the actual amount depends on your debts, future financial obligations, and family needs. Use this calculator to get a personalized estimate.
What's the difference between term and whole life insurance?
Term life insurance provides coverage for a specific period (e.g., 10, 20, or 30 years) and is generally more affordable. Whole life insurance provides lifelong coverage and builds cash value but costs significantly more.
When should I buy life insurance?
The best time to buy life insurance is when you're young and healthy, as premiums are lower. Consider purchasing life insurance when you have dependents, a mortgage, or other financial obligations that would burden others if you were gone.

📖 Our Story

MoneyCalcPro was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to make financial calculations and planning accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical expertise or financial background. In a world where financial decisions can have life-changing consequences, we believe that everyone deserves access to accurate, easy-to-use tools that help them make informed choices.

The platform was created by Subhan Ali, who recognized that many people struggle with basic financial calculations when making important decisions about loans, investments, savings, and more. Whether it's calculating monthly EMI payments for a home loan, understanding the true cost of borrowing, or planning for retirement, having the right tools can make all the difference.

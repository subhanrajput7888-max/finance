About Text to Speech

Our Text to Speech tool uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis capabilities to convert written text into natural-sounding speech. This tool is perfect for proofreading, accessibility, language learning, and creating audio content.

Features:

  • Multiple voice options
  • Adjustable speech rate
  • Pitch control
  • Play, pause, and stop controls

Use Cases:

  • Proofread documents aloud
  • Accessibility for visually impaired
  • Language learning practice
  • Content creation assistance

Note: This tool uses the Web Speech API built into your browser. Available voices and quality may vary depending on your browser and operating system.

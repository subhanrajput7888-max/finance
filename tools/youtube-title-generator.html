Tips for Great YouTube Titles

  • Keep it under 60 characters - Titles get cut off in search results
  • Include your main keyword - Helps with SEO and discovery
  • Use numbers - "5 Ways to..." or "Top 10..." perform well
  • Create curiosity - Make viewers want to click
  • Avoid clickbait - Deliver on what your title promises

Title Formulas That Work

  • How to [Achieve Result] in [Timeframe]
  • [Number] Ways to [Solve Problem]
  • The Ultimate Guide to [Topic]
  • [Number] [Topic] You Need to Know
  • I Tried [Thing] for [Time] - Here's What Happened

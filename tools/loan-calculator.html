What is a Loan Calculator?

A Loan Calculator is an essential financial tool that helps you determine the total cost of borrowing money. It calculates your monthly payments, total amount payable, and the total interest you'll pay over the loan period. Whether you're considering a personal loan, car loan, or business loan, this calculator helps you make informed decisions.

How Does It Work?

The loan calculator uses the amortization formula to calculate your monthly payments. It considers three key factors:

  • Loan Amount: The principal amount you're borrowing
  • Interest Rate: The annual percentage rate charged by the lender
  • Loan Tenure: The repayment period in months

Formula

Monthly Payment = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]

Where:

  • P = Principal Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
  • N = Number of Monthly Installments

Example Calculation

Personal Loan Scenario:

  • Loan Amount = $50,000
  • Annual Interest Rate = 9%
  • Tenure = 36 months (3 years)
  • Monthly Payment = $1,592.79
  • Total Payment = $57,340.44
  • Total Interest = $7,340.44

Benefits

  • Understand total loan cost before borrowing
  • Compare different loan offers easily
  • Plan your monthly budget effectively
  • Assess loan affordability
  • Make informed financial decisions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between EMI and Loan Calculator?

They use the same formula. EMI Calculator focuses on monthly payments, while Loan Calculator shows the complete cost breakdown.

Does the calculator include processing fees?

No, this calculator shows only principal and interest. Processing fees and other charges are additional.

Can I use this for any type of loan?

Yes, this calculator works for personal loans, car loans, business loans, and other installment loans.

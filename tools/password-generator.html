Password Security Tips

  • Use at least 12 characters - Longer passwords are harder to crack
  • Mix character types - Combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid personal information - Don't use names, birthdays, or common words
  • Use unique passwords - Never reuse passwords across different accounts
  • Use a password manager - Store and manage your passwords securely

How It Works

Our password generator uses cryptographically secure random number generation to create truly random passwords. The passwords are generated locally in your browser and are never transmitted over the internet.

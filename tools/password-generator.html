What is a Password Generator?

A Password Generator is a tool that creates strong, random passwords to protect your online accounts. With cyber threats increasing daily, using weak or predictable passwords is one of the biggest security risks you can take.

Our generator creates truly random passwords that are virtually impossible to crack, ensuring your accounts stay secure. You can customize the length and character types to meet any website's requirements.

Why Use Strong Passwords?

Weak passwords are the #1 cause of data breaches. Strong passwords:

  • Protect your accounts from unauthorized access
  • Prevent identity theft and fraud
  • Safeguard sensitive data like banking information
  • Meet security requirements for work and school

Password Strength Guidelines

Minimum Requirements:
✓ At least 12 characters long
✓ Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
✓ Include numbers and special characters
✓ No dictionary words or personal info
✓ Unique for each account

Example Passwords

Strong Password Examples:

  • 16 characters: K#9mP$vL2@xQ7nR!
  • 20 characters: T8&fW@3jN#pY5mL$xQ9z!
  • Time to crack: Millions of years with current technology

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should my password be?

Minimum 12 characters, but 16+ is recommended for important accounts. Longer passwords are exponentially harder to crack.

Is it safe to use an online password generator?

Our generator runs 100% in your browser - passwords are never sent to any server. However, for maximum security, use offline generators or password managers.

Should I use the same password everywhere?

Never! Use unique passwords for each account. If one site is breached, your other accounts remain safe. Consider using a password manager to keep track.

Password Security Tips

  1. Use a password manager to store passwords
  2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  3. Never share passwords via email or text
  4. Change passwords immediately after data breaches
  5. Don't use personal info (birthdays, names) in passwords
  6. Use passphrases for easier memorization

Our EMI Calculator helps you plan loans effectively:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Enter Loan Amount: Total amount you want to borrow (e.g., $25,000 for a car)
  2. Interest Rate: Annual interest rate offered by your bank (e.g., 7.5%)
  3. Loan Tenure: Repayment period in years or months (e.g., 5 years)
  4. Click Calculate: Instantly see your monthly EMI, total interest, and total payment

What You'll Learn:

  • Monthly EMI amount to budget for
  • Total interest you'll pay over the loan period
  • Complete payment breakdown (principal vs. interest)
  • How changing tenure affects your EMI

Pro Tip: Try different combinations of loan amount and tenure to find an EMI that fits your monthly budget comfortably!

Yes! Our BMI Calculator is your first step toward better health awareness.

What BMI Tells You:

  • Underweight: BMI below 18.5 - May need to gain weight
  • Normal Weight: BMI 18.5-24.9 - Healthy range
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9 - May benefit from weight loss
  • Obese: BMI 30+ - Should consult healthcare provider

Important Notes:

  • BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic measure
  • Doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition
  • Athletes may have high BMI due to muscle, not fat
  • Always consult healthcare professionals for personalized advice

Use our calculator regularly to track changes and monitor your health journey alongside proper diet and exercise.

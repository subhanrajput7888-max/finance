Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

At MoneyCalcPro, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We do not collect any personal information from users of our calculators. All calculations are performed locally in your browser, and no data is transmitted to our servers.

Review Data: When you submit a review, we store your name and review message in your browser's local storage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Automatically Collected Information: Like most websites, we may collect certain information automatically when you visit our site, including your browser type, device type, operating system, and pages visited. This information is used solely for improving our website's performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal data through our calculators, we do not use, sell, rent, or lease any personal information to third parties. The only information we process includes:

  • Reviews you voluntarily submit (stored locally on your device)
  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve our website
  • Contact form submissions when you reach out to us directly

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Review data is stored in your browser's local storage. This means the data never leaves your device. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

Calculations: All calculator inputs and outputs remain in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any financial or personal data you enter into our calculators.

Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect our website. However, please note that no electronic transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Image hosting services (e.g., Unsplash for blog images)
  • Analytics services to understand website usage patterns
  • CDN services to improve website loading speed

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and improve our services. Cookies are files with small amounts of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some portions of our website may not function properly.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from children under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of processing your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service

Since we do not collect personal data through our calculators, these rights primarily apply to any contact form submissions or communications you send us directly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

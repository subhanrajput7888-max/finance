Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we protect your data.

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

Your Privacy is Protected

All tools process data directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to MoneyCalcPro ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services.

By using MoneyCalcPro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Don't Collect

We take a privacy-first approach. Here's what we do NOT collect:

  • Files you process: All file processing (PDFs, images, etc.) happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Personal identification: We don't require account creation or personal information to use our tools.
  • Browsing history: We don't track which tools you use or how you use them.
  • Device fingerprints: We don't collect unique device identifiers.

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our services:

3.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Log Data: When you visit our website, our servers may automatically log standard data provided by your web browser, including your IP address, browser type and version, visited pages, time and date of visit, and time spent on pages.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device type, operating system, and screen size for optimizing your experience.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode) and to improve site functionality.

3.2 Contact Form Information

If you contact us through our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Your message content

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To monitor website usage and detect technical issues
  • To protect against unauthorized access and abuse

5. Client-Side Processing

A key feature of MoneyCalcPro is that all file processing happens directly in your web browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your files are never uploaded to our servers
  • We cannot access, view, or store your files
  • Processing happens entirely on your device
  • Your data remains completely private

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: We may use privacy-focused analytics tools to understand website traffic patterns. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information.
  • Advertising: We may display ads through Google AdSense or similar networks. These services may use cookies to serve relevant ads.
  • CDN: We use Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to serve static files like fonts and icons.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality, such as remembering your theme preference.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (if enabled).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by our advertising partners to serve relevant ads (if you have consented).

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Limited data collection minimizes exposure
  • No storage of processed files or sensitive data

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
